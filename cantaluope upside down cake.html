CANTALUOPE UPSIDE DOWN CAKE

A Short History of Cantaluope Upside Down Cake

The origins of the Cantaluope upside down cake date back hundreds of years, when people would cook on cast-iron skillets over open fires. When they wanted a sweet treat, they would line the bottom of the pans with fruit, and pour batter on top.This dish was made by Ammu Abhirami in Cook with Comali season 3.This dish were given top rated by the chefs
