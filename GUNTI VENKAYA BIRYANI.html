GUNTI VENKAYA BIRIYANI

A BIRIYANI WITH MINOR DISH

Baingan Biryani or Brinjal Biryani is also known as guthi vankaya biryani in Telugu is speciality biryani from Andra Cuisine. This biryani rice is similar to any other biryani but the stuffed eggplant in this biryani is something that asks you to eat more and more.
